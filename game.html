LEARN THE BASICS

There's plenty to discover about Nasalmi, so let's begin with the basics. Check out the guide below to get acquainted with the most popular gameplay mode

SURVIVE THE WAVES

10 waves await you in each level full of monsters that will make you sweat.

THE BUTTON

Press the button to start the waves. But be careful, this will close all the doors, and you will lose life if you want to open them again.

INCREASE YOUR STATS

To overcome your enemies, increase your stats picking up Power-Ups.

THE TREASURE ROOM

Every level, a Treasure room will spawn that contains 1 random Power-up. Pray for a good one and lets Start killing monsters.

MANAGE YOUR GOLD

If you are lucky, monsters will drop pickup items, save money and become more powerfull.

THE SHOP

Go into the shop and use that hard earned gold you have to get your favourite Power-Up. But be careful, make sure you are strong enough to survive until the next Shop!.

DEFEAT THE HARDEST ENEMIES

With every level, harder enemies will Spawn, including Hard Bosses.

THE END

No matter how strong u are, eventually you are going to die. But dont worry, keep playing and you will get better and better, and maybe, just maybe, you will reach the top of the rankings.
